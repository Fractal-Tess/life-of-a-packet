## Wi-Fi: The Local Wireless Magic

Wi-Fi is like an invisible bridge connecting devices:

- Uses radio waves (2.4GHz and 5GHz bands)
- IEEE 802.11 standards (a/b/g/n/ac/ax)
- Access points manage connections
- Range affected by obstacles and interference

## Mobile Networks: The Cellular Symphony

Mobile networks are like a vast orchestra of cell towers:

- Generations: 3G, 4G LTE, 5G
- Cell towers provide coverage areas
- Handoff between towers as you move
- 5G promises faster speeds and lower latency

## Wireless Security: The Protective Spells

Wireless networks need special protection:

- Encryption protocols (WEP, WPA, WPA2, WPA3)
- Hidden SSIDs
- MAC address filtering
- Regular firmware updates

## Signal Propagation: The Invisible Waves

Wireless signals are affected by:
- Distance from the access point
- Physical obstacles (walls, furniture)
- Interference from other devices
- Signal reflection and absorption
